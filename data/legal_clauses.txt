**Standard Legal Clauses**

**1. Subrogation Clause**
   - In the event of any payment under this policy, the Company shall be subrogated to all the Insured's rights of recovery against any person or organization.

**2. Fraud and Misrepresentation Clause**
   - This policy is void if any insured has intentionally concealed or misrepresented any material fact or circumstance relating to this insurance.

**3. Cancellation Clause**
   - The named insured may cancel this policy by mailing to the Company written notice stating when thereafter the cancellation shall be effective. The Company may cancel this policy by mailing to the named insured written notice.

**4. Action Against Company Clause**
   - No action shall lie against the Company unless, as a condition precedent thereto, there shall have been full compliance with all the terms of this policy.
